How BinCoin Works

A simple, gamified system that makes waste management visible and rewarding for everyone.

1

Report Waste

Citizens snap photos of waste via our app or WhatsApp bot. Reports appear instantly on our live map.

2

Earn BinCoins

Verified reports earn you BinCoins - our eco-reward tokens that can be redeemed for real benefits.

3

Redeem Rewards

Use your BinCoins at participating vendors, for municipal services, or in community challenges.
